Fylrobot commands for CollabVM:

!help - Help.
!nousername - removes your username and fills it with ( )
!spam - Spams a phrase in collabvm chat.
!rapidname - Changes name to numbers.
!custommessage - Makes Axcelubot say a custom message.
!screenshot - Takes screenshot of VM.
!turnspam - Spams turn.
!disconnect - Reloads page and disconnects.
!record - Record VM screen.
!customscript - Add a script from a URL.
!darkmode - Adds dark mode to your screen. (made by kekero on CollabVM.)
!autotype - Autotypes a phrase inside the VM.
!voteyes/voteno - Votes in a VM reset for you.
